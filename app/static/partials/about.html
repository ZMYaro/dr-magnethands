Story

You are a team of superheroes who must stop Dr. Magnethands from launching his rocket-powered radioactive castle on the moon into the White House on Christmas Eve.

How to play

After you join a game, you will be asked to enter eight things. Those Things can be whatever you want—names, identities, abilities, props, plot devices, or anything else you can think of. Once each player has entered eight Things, the game can begin.

Each player will be assigned four Things at random. It is up to you how those define your character. If you get “a rusty spork”, your character could own a rusty spork, have had a traumatic experience with a rusty spork, or even literally be a rusty spork. Your call.

The GM/host starts the story. The host can draw from the remaining Things at any time, and they shape the plot. At each phase, players must use their Things to defeat Dr. Magnethands's henchmen and, eventually, Dr. Magnethands himself. If you run out of Things, you die. If you get to the end and defeat Dr. Magnethands, you win!

If you want, you can also read the original (tabletop) rules.
